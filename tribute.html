LATA MANGESHKAR


~“I sing because it brings me immense happiness.”

QUICK FACTS

  • Born: September 28, 1929, in Indore, India.
  • Debut: Started singing in 1942 with the Marathi film "Kiti Hasaal."
  • Breakthrough: "Aayega Aanewala" from Mahal (1949).
  • Language: Recorded songs in over 25 languages.
  • Record: Guinness World Record for the most recorded artist.
  • Songs: Estimated to have recorded over 25,000 songs.
  • Awards: Bharat Ratna, Padma Bhushan, Padma Vibhushan, Dadasaheb Phalke Award.
  • Family: Asha Bhosle, Hridaynath Mangeshkar, Usha Mangeshkar.
  • Retirement: Announced retirement from singing in 2020.
